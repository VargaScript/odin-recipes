Tacos

Tacos is a delicious meal made with the finest ingredients of the world.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Grated cheese
  • Diced onions
  • Minced garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges
    1. Choose your protein:
      • Ground beef
      • Shredded chicken
      • Grilled vegetables (for a vegetarian option)
    2. Cook the protein with taco seasoning until fully cooked.
    3. Prepare taco toppings:
      • Chopped lettuce
      • Diced tomatoes
      • Shredded cheese
      • Diced onions
      • Chopped cilantro
      • Sour cream
      • Salsa or pico de gallo
      • Sliced jalape√±os (optional)
    4. Warm the tortillas:
      • Place them in a dry skillet or microwave briefly.
      • Alternatively, heat them in the oven wrapped in foil.
    5. Assemble the tacos:
      • Place a spoonful of the cooked protein on a tortilla.
      • Add your desired toppings.
      • Drizzle with salsa, sour cream, or hot sauce.
