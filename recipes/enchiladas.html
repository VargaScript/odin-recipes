Enchiladas

Enchiladas is a delicious meal made with the finest ingredients of the world.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Mole
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Sour Cream
  • Avocado
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook and shred the chicken or beef.
  3. Chop onions, bell peppers, and cilantro.
  4. Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  5. Spread a small amount of enchilada sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Assemble the enchiladas: place a spoonful of meat, onions, bell peppers, and cheese in each tortilla. Roll tightly and place seam side down in the baking dish.
  7. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas.
  8. Sprinkle additional cheese on top.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
  11. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with sour cream, salsa, avocado, and lime wedges on the side.
